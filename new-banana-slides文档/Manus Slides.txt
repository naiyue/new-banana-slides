Tips for Better Presentations

Be specific about audience:
✅ “Technical audience familiar with ML”
✅ “Business executives, no technical background”
❌ “General audience”
Define the goal:
✅ “Convince investors to fund us”
✅ “Educate students on the basics”
❌ “Present information”
Specify length and timing:
✅ “15 slides for 20-minute presentation”
✅ “30 slides for 1-hour workshop”
❌ “Make some slides”
Let Manus research:
✅ “Research current market trends and include them”
❌ Providing all content yourself
Common Questions

Can I edit slides after creation? Yes. Download the .pptx file and edit in any presentation software. How long does it take? 5-15 minutes depending on complexity. Simple 10-slide deck: ~5 min. Complex 40-slide research presentation: ~15 min. Can Manus use my company template? Yes. Upload your .pptx template file. What if I need changes? Ask Manus to make changes through conversation, or edit the PowerPoint file directly. Can Manus create charts from my data? Yes. Upload your data (CSV, Excel), and Manus will create appropriate visualizations. What languages are supported? All major languages. Specify in your request: “Create in Spanish…”
